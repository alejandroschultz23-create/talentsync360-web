# DESIGN.md - TalentSync360 Engine v2.0

## Vision: High-Speed Talent Curation
This design system transforms TalentSync360 from a traditional agency model into a high-octane talent engine. The aesthetic is **"Premium SaaS Engineering"**: deep, technical, and extremely fast.

## Core Aesthetic Tokens
- **Backbone (Background):** `Slate-950` (#020617). A technical black that provides infinite depth.
- **Action (Primary):** `Indigo-600` (#4f46e5). Represents the precision and engineering core of the matching engine.
- **Success/Growth (Secondary):** `Emerald-500` (#10b981). Represents the speed of delivery and the health of the talent pool.
- **Typography:** **Geist**. Chosen for its geometric precision and modern readability.

## The Island Test (IA & GEO Optimization)
To optimize for AI context (Gemini, Claude) and direct user engagement:
- **No Vague Pronouns:** Eliminate "we", "us", "our".
- **Direct Declarations:** Use factual statements. 
- **Example:** Instead of "We find the best talent for you," use "TalentSync360 Engine delivers curated shortlists in 72 hours."

## Layout Principles
1. **Dynamic Grid:** No fixed vertical silos. Talent is displayed in a fluid, skill-first grid.
2. **Glassmorphism:** Use `backdrop-blur-md` and `bg-slate-900/80` for floating elements to create layers of information without adding visual clutter.
3. **Micro-animations:** Subtle transitions (200ms) on skill tags and talent cards to indicate real-time engine activity.

## E-E-A-T Authority Metrics
- **80%** Talent gap in Spain.
- **60%** Savings for US/EU Startups.
- **95%** Fill rate in under 30 days.
- **72h** Shortlist delivery window.
